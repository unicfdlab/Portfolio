Ivanov Alexander

Alexander graduated from Moscow State University (MSU), the "Physics" department, «Mathematical physics» program in 2019. Currently he is a PhD student at Keldysh Institute of Applied Mathematics.

He is an author and co-author of about 5 papers and 3 conference abstracts on the topics of hydrodynamics simulation and shallow water approximation (2 papers are included in the Scopus database).

Professional interests: hydro-, gasdynamics; numerical simulation; quasi-hydro -, quasi-gas-dynamic algorithms; finite volume method; shallow water equations; impurity transfer in shallow water.
